1. Guess the hidden number from 1-20

2. Enter your guess in the input field and then click check.

3. If your guess matches with hidden number then the hidden number will be displayed and you will win the game.

4. If your guess becomes wrong then you will get a message on right side of the window to help you to guess another number which should either be greater than or less than your previous guess.

5. This process continues till you either guess a correct number or your all 20 chances of guess will over.

6. You can always start a new game by clicking on "Again" button.
